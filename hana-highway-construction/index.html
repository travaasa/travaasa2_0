HANA HIGHWAY RESURFACING
PROJECT NO. 360B-01-12M

 
Road closures on the Hana Highway Resurfacing project will commence on Wednesday, October 1, 2014 to October 31, 2014. These closures will occur between Mile Post 19 and Mile Post 24. Sections of the Highway will be closed at the following approximate times, Monday thru Friday, except on Holidays:
 
9:00 a.m. – 11:00 a.m.
12:00 p.m. – 2:00 p.m.
3:00 p.m. – 5:00 p.m.
 
Contra-flow traffic will be implemented in areas that are deemed safe and reasonable when available. If personal emergencies occur, please notify the traffic control personnel at the beginning of the road closure station, and they will make arrangements to escort the personnel through the work area. Reminder that this will be for emergencies only, as there are great impacts to the work and could cause extension of the working days, extending the project duration.
