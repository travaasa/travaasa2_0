Adventure

STAND UP PADDLING – Hana, Maui

Come join us for a stand up paddleboard adventure in beautiful Hana Bay. In addition to gaining a new skill, you will learn the history and origins of paddlboarding, including how it was used for transportation, fishing as well as recreational purposes.

POWER PADDLING – Hana, Maui

Hawaiian Outrigger Canoe Paddling is currently one of the most popular endurance sports. In this 90-minute session, you will learn an efficient technique essential to enjoyable and fast outrigger canoeing. A welcome 'oli (chant) sets the tone for an incredible trek into Hana Bay, and is followed by stories and lessons from our local adventure guides of the history, myths and legends unique to Hana.

Fitness

BOOST YOUR CARDIO - JUMP ROPE INTERVALS – Austin

Dread those cardio days? Mix it up with jump roping and a variety of other plyometric exercises that utilize the BOSU ball and other small props to blast away fat—you'll torch as many as 700 calories in less than 45 minutes. In this intense yet fun-filled class, you’ll stay mentally focused and physically challenged, all throughout your workout.

BOOST YOUR CARDIO — BOOTCAMP – Hana, Maui

Get ready for intervals that make your heart rate soar. Boot camp circuit training delivers maximum results in a minimum amount of time, all while promoting all-around fitness. We’ll challenge your body and mind as we mix up the moves and the intensity of the workout to keep you motivated. Plus you'll be surrounded by 360° views of stunning Hana scenery, then end the circuit with a trip to the beach!

POWER UP METABOLIC WORKOUT – Austin

Get the “biggest bang for your buck" using your stabilization, balance, power exercises and fun props to improve the capacity of your energy systems. Alternating intense exercises with rest intervals, you can complete a large amount of work in minimal time. Get ready for metabolic training that increases your capacity for high-intensity exercise while torching calories.

VITAL BREATH WORK – Austin

Breathing is vital to our wellbeing and the proper function of our muscular and organ systems. Learn the various types of breath and how to connect with your inner body. The result is greater awareness of your breathing patterns plus improved functionality, range of motion and state of mind.

BOOST YOUR GABA YOGA THERAPY – Austin

Yoga, the popular joint-loosening, muscle-toning exercise method, cranks up brain levels of gamma-aminobutyric acid (GABA), a neurotransmitter that helps take the edge off anxiety and bust up blue moods. Get into the present moment and into the physical body by practicing yoga poses that activate your vagus nerve, enabling a whole chain of all-around good feelings.

VIVA FOOTWORKS – Austin

We constantly put our feet to work without giving them proper care. With this class, you will learn the proper massage and mechanics that can help strengthen muscles and heal aches and pains associated with overworked feet, aiding mobility and posture.

EXPLORE THE VIGOR OF PILATES – Austin

Beneficial to your overall health, mobility, strength and power, Pilates method works to activate the inner unit of the axial body, helping to develop the deeper muscles of your core. With proper breath and form, just one Pilates class can improve your posture, as well as increase your stamina and vigor.

TAI CHI AND ENERGY – Austin

Learn the fundamentals of Tai Chi and the energy centers of your physical body and the world around you. Leave with a heightened awareness of both your physical vitality and your spiritual energy.

Salsa Dance & Invigorate – Austin

Dance to the festive Salsa rhythms and invigorate your body as it moves to this fun, energetic and happy music.

ZUMBA POWER – Austin

Zumba is a revolutionary fitness experience that takes you on an unforgettable, exhilarating journey to a healthier and happier lifestyle. Jam-packed with steaming hot dance moves, pulsating Latin and international rhythms and feel-it-to-the-core routines, this invigorating dance-fitness party will have you movin' and groovin' all class long.

DETOX YOGA – Austin

Leap into spring with an invigorating and sweaty Hatha flow class that lightens up your step. This class will infuse twists to ring out any sluggishness in both our minds and bodies so that we may be porous to soak up all the vitality that has to spring offer.

YOGA – SHINE WITH VITALITY – Hana, Maui

Salute the new year with sun salutations. Highlighting individual postures, and focusing on various nuances of the sun salutation, this practice will deepen the foundation of individual postures, while building to a fluid, repetitive sequence that strengthens and revitalizes the body, mind and spirit.

Culinary

JUICING FOR VITALITY – Austin & Hana, Maui

Excellent for physically active people, these energy-boosting juicing recipes can provide a boost of energy and vital nutrients, promoting muscle growth and improved physical performance.

VITAL HEALTHY EATING HABITS – Austin

Culture

Learn the history and culture of the `ukulele as well as the basic chords necessary to play a song or two. Enjoy this 45-minute session as you play a centuries old instrument.

Learn how to string and twine fragrant, fresh flowers and ti leaves (also known as the "goodluck plant") into a beautiful lei to wear during your stay.
