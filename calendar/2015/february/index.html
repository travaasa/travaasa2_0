Culinary

APHRODISIAC CULINARY DEMONSTRATION – Austin

An aphrodisiac is a substance that increases sexual desire. The name comes from Aphrodite, the Greek goddess of sexuality and love. During this culinary demonstration, our chefs will teach you the different effects of certain ingredients such as arugula, basil, fennel, carrots and avocado, while also creating an unforgettable recipe for you to enjoy at home...or in the boudoir.

BREAKFAST IN BED CULINARY DEMONSTRATION – Austin

Have you ever desired to surprise that special someone with an unforgettable breakfast in bed? Develop the skills you need to give you confidence in the kitchen as we demonstrate amazing, yet simple, breakfast-in-bed recipes. Our Chefs will guide you through each step of the recipe and give you tips on culinary techniques, such as poaching eggs and how to make hollandaise seem simple. This also gives you the opportunity to spend time with our Chef.
