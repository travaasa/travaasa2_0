Looking to the future, we will be undergoing a series of renovation projects. While our next chapter is sure to be an exciting one, the growing pains will, unfortunately, have a period of extensive construction and keep us from delivering our full Travaasa experience.

As a result, our resort will be closed from September 2017 through August 2018.

During this time, we will be adding additional spaces for new experiences, enhancing our rooms, and – believe it or not – making our pool even more amazing than it already is by adding another one (no, really).

We apologize to our guests we will miss during this period and are available to answer any questions you may have.  Thank you for your patience with us and for helping be a part of our story. We hope you can join us again upon our reopening and look forward to continuing to create new memories.

Please feel free to reach out to us at austin@travaasa.com or (855) 868-7282.
