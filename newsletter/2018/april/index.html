INTRODUCING A WORLD-CLASS RESORT IN FABULOUS

Aloha...

...is a fine Hawaiian word of greeting, love and wish for your well-being. it is particularly useful here in Hana, Maui. In Hana we are fortunate to be twice-blessed; we live in an environment of rare beauty, and we are able to share it with friends.

At Hotel Hana-Maui we are convinced it is like no other area in the world. We hope that after a few days to become acclimated, you too will feel the same, and that you will find it to be a total environmental experience.

–Paul Fagan
