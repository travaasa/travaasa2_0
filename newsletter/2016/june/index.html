Good Libations

June is Man Month at Travaasa, and our Beverage Manager and Sommelier, Edward Morgan has curated a Scotch-tasting experience that even Ron Burgundy would approve. You'll leave an expert on the difference between Whisky, Whiskey, Scotch and Bourbon. If Scotch is not for your palette, Edward's got you covered with summer wine and beer recommendations—and a spicy cocktail, too! Bottoms up.

New Men's Treatments in Hana

New rooms, new restaurant, and now—new spa experiences! In case you needed more ways to relax in Heavenly Hana, our spa team has created a few. In honor of Man Month, we'll be introducing a Kane treatment designed specifically for the guys on our newly refreshed spa menu. One thing you won't see... an off-the-menu treatment to request once you've completed driving the Road to Hana. Shhh it's a secret.

Working On Our Short Game

Our newest activity is summertime-ready! We've got disc golf available now in both Austin and Hana. Perfect for hanging out with friends, our portable golf holes can be checked out with our experience team and taken throughout the property to play anytime! Whether it's for bragging rights or just for fun, we highly recommend an afternoon spent tossing the disc.

Can't Stop, Won't Stop

What has two wheels and is so much fun? Us. In Austin, our Bike Pump Track continues to be a fan favorite. Our resident Bike Guide, Jeff Compton, has created an experience that extends far beyond the track. Whether it's challenging guests to try something they didn't think they could do, or taking cycling enthusiasts on rides throughout Austin, the bike program is on a roll (no pun intended).
