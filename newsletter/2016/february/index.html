Did someone say chocolate?

A wise person once said indulgence is the key to happiness. Okay, maybe we said that. Either way, you might as well do it properly. Visit us in Austin and spend a decadent afternoon learning how to make handmade chocolates with Chef Marko. Or if pampered is what you prefer, stop by The Spa to sample our newest treatments.

"I... have made fire!"

Our newest experience in Hana has us channeling our inner Pele (the Hawaiian Goddess of Fire). The Fire by Friction class demonstrates how to master the primitive survival skill of firestarting. Our guides teach the basics of the ancient Bow Drill and leave you feeling like Bear Grylls is your long lost brother.

Perfect Pairings in Austin

The Preserve Kitchen + Bar in Austin is excited to introduce our "Perfect Pairings" wine flights! Created by our Sommelier & Beverage Manager, Edward Morgan, the interactive offering is a fun way to make your dinner into an entire culinary experience. With each course, Edward will select the best varietals to accompany Chef Ben's dishes. Now that deserves a toast!

Stretches for the Heart

February is so much more than romance here at Travaasa. We use this month as a time to make sure we're focused on keeping our heart healthy and happy. To that end, we've compiled tips from our yoga and meditation experts to help do just that! We've got stretches, poses and all the advice you need to make sure you're taking time to focus on your heart.
