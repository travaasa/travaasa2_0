Island Fresh

Fun fact: Maui has over 800 farms throughout the island and it just so happens that Hana is home to a few of our favorites. From the delicious fruits at ONO Organic Farms to the sweet treats from Hana Gold, our neighboring cacao farm, we've got your guide for how to enjoy Harvest month, Hana-style.

Plant. Cook. Eat.

While the Chef Ben is busy creating new dishes with Kim's harvest, Chef Marko has a new class making its debut this month. In his Plant-Based Cooking demonstration. Learn about the many nutritional benefits of the Fall bounty and how to incorporate these fresh ingredients for a dining experience that channels The Preserve Kitchen at home!

No bad side.

In addition to tending crops, caring for chickens and crafting herbal remedies, our farmer is a photographer, too. To celebrate all that she does—both in the fields and behind the lens—we want to showcase the fruits of her labor this Harvest Month. Take a look at her work... capturing her work.
