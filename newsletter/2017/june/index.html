Hemingway’s Hana

Ernest Hemingway's quest for new adventures and expeditions earned him the status of travel legend. His stories are inspired by journeys that span the globe, so when Ernie named Hana’s Hamoa Beach as the “Best in the World” we know he actually meant the entire world. It turns out, he wasn’t the only one that found our little Hana Town to be a muse for his works...

"The Most Amazing Resort"

Travaasa Hana was the most amazing resort my husband and I have ever experienced. After a long drive to Hana, staying here for two nights was absolutely perfect. It is so tranquil and relaxing. The property is perfect. The staff was beyond friendly, and so accommodating. The spa was just lovely. We had massages, and enjoyed the saunas, individual cold pools and outdoor jacuzzi. Take the time to appreciate the spa property! We also took paddle board lessons and our instructor was a doll! We did a package, so we ate at the restaurant onsite and thought everything was delicious - all meals. The rooms were private and relaxing - we loved that there were no TVs! Once the sun went down, it was just us and the ocean. The weather was absolutely perfect, so we slept with the doors open, listening to the ocean. One morning, I hiked up to Fagan's Cross - one of my favorite experiences in Maui. Don't give up looking for the Red Sand Beach. It's right there and I could not get to it! Just ask! We will return, and i would recommend a stay to anyone in need of some relaxation.
– Jennifer Y. • Lakewood, CO
ON THE HORIZON

July is Fly Month

The panoramic ocean view from a lanai is something truly spectacular, but the view from above is simply breathtaking! Journeying along the Hana Highway, seeing the many waterfalls and roadside treasures is something on every traveler’s bucket list, no doubt. However, to fully take in all the natural beauty that this experience has to offer, we recommend taking things up a notch. As in up in the sky, amongst the clouds and over the coastline. July is Fly Month at Travaasa, so prepare for takeoff.

August is Dive Month

To dive? Or not to dive? That is never a question for us. Whether you’re going full tilt into one of our incredible infinity pools or feet-first into a new adventure, we’ve got plenty of ways to celebrate Dive Month in August. For beginners that just want to dip a toe in, we’ve got snorkel gear, SUP and aquatic spa experiences. For pros, we’ve got tips from our very-own expert diver, Chef Bella. Because when she’s not in the kitchen creating a new masterpiece, she’s basically a mermaid.
