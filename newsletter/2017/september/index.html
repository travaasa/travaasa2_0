5 Tips for the Road to Hana

With over 600 turns, and 54 bridges, the Hana Highway is much more than just a road—it's an experience. You'll be hard-pressed to pick a favorite stop along the awe-inspiring drive, but a waterfall or two is sure to be up there on your list! Dive into one of the seemingly infinite numbers of falls lining the road to Travaasa, and enjoy their beauty along with their power. A local favorite is Wailua Falls, located at Mile Marker 45. This is a wonderful fall that has a short path on the right side of the bridge leading you right below the falls for a dip! Just be careful on the often slippery rocks, and you're good to go! Hokulea Homecoming

In 2015, Hokule'a celebrated 40 years of voyaging. Launched from the sacred shores of Hawaii in 1975, she helped begin a new generation of renewal for Hawaii's people. This month, Hokule'a returned home to Hawaii after circumnavigating the globe without the use of modern technology. "The Most Amazing Resort"

Travaasa Hana was the most amazing resort my husband and I have ever experienced. After a long drive to Hana, staying here for two nights was absolutely perfect. It is so tranquil and relaxing. The property is perfect. The staff was beyond friendly and so accommodating. The spa was just lovely. We had massages and enjoyed the saunas, individual cold pools and outdoor jacuzzi. Take the time to appreciate the spa property! We also took paddle board lessons and our instructor was a doll! We did a package, so we ate at the restaurant on site and thought everything was delicious—all meals. The rooms were private and relaxing—we loved that there were no TVs! Once the sun went down, it was just us and the ocean. The weather was absolutely perfect, so we slept with the doors open, listening to the ocean. One morning, I hiked up to Fagan's Cross - one of my favorite experiences in Maui. Don't give up looking for the Red Sand Beach. It's right there and I could not get to it! Just ask! We will return, and I would recommend a stay to anyone in need of some relaxation.
– Jennifer Y. • Lakewood, CO
