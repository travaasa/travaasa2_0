Cultural Wanderings: Wananalua Canoeing in Hana 

The best way to see Hana Bay? By outrigger canoe, of course. Discover secluded beaches, bays and sandbars and explore gorgeous sea cliffs and caves, while getting a full body workout in our four-person, custom-made canoe.

The Spa at Travaasa Austin named a Top 10 Organic Spa

Organic Spa Magazine recently honored Austin's spa, naming it a Top 10 Organic Spa worldwide. The award praises our authentically natural and organic products, certified organic cotton sheets, locally-sourced food from the property's organic farm and its in-house irrigation system.
