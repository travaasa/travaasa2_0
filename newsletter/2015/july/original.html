ON THE HORIZON

August is Dive Month

Dive Month at Travaasa is the perfect excuse to sit poolside, overlooking Hill Country in Austin or exploring Hamoa Beach by canoe in Hana. September is when the summer starts to wind down and the kids go back to school, making it an ideal time to try new things at Travaasa. Discover Month focuses on pushing oneself to new limits and trying new experiences. We recommend getting into the kitchen with a cooking or cocktail demonstration, or spending some time on the farm.
