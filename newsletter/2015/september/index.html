Ocean Bungalows Sneak Peek

This year we began a property-wide restoration project that will enhance our guest rooms in Hana. With that, we're excited to introduce our Ocean Bungalows (formerly Sea Ranch Cottages). The renewed rooms will showcase durable Ipe Wood lanais, custom furnishings and local photography. Take a peek of what is to come!

Meet Our New Experience Concierge

Hatchet Throwing, Pilates, Chickens, Swimming, Massages... there is a lot to cover during a visit. To help, we'd like to introduce you to Andrea Nathan, our new Experience Concierge. Andrea is here to assist with scheduling, planning and making the most out of a visit to Travaasa Austin. Get to know her a little better.

The Mind + Body Triathlon

Our newest experience helps discover deeper levels of strength. Our Mind + Body Triathlon focuses on connecting one's energy with nature in every way possible. From meditation, to strength training, to yoga, the class tests focus, raises self-awareness... and also serves up a pretty tough workout.

ON THE HORIZON

October is Harvest Month

We're taking this month literally. There is a lot of growth happening on our farm in Austin with our new chicken tractors and fresh new ingredients making their debut on the menu at The Preserve.
