Fall's Latest (Farm) Accessory: Chicken Tractors

Harvest Month is one of the greatest times of the year for our farm crew! And much like the runways in New York, Paris and Milan, we've got a new accessory making its debut at Travaasa Austin. Introducing: chicken tractors, handmade by our very own farmer, Joe. They do much more than look pretty.

Autumnal Libations

No pumpkin spice here, folks. Our creative culinary team is incorporating the farm's seasonal bounty into new menus, dishes and (most importantly) drinks! We've got a few new adult beverages to warm you up as you enjoy the best of Harvest Month. Get in the mood for sweater weather with our Warm Welcome cocktail. Try the recipe.

Get Your Roll On

It's hard to say who loves Harvest Month more, our farmers or our culinary team! Inspired by all of Kim and Joe's hard work, our chefs have incorporated seasonal ingredients into our experiences as well. Join Chef Marko in this month's cooking demonstrations to learn how to make his latest creation, Nori Rolls, to try at home. Bonus: it's not just delicious, it's healthy, too.
