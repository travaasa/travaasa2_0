Each year, The Travel Channel awards one lucky viewer a dream travel itinerary. "The Trip: 2015" says Aloha to the Hawaiian Islands and highlights Maui's ultimate retreat at Travaasa Hana. Watch it January 26 at 8pm ET/PT.

Shuck & Dive for Immunity

It's oyster season and we've got the scoop on preparing these flavorful, health-boosting shellfish at home. Get shucking with Chef Marko Ellinger's Hemp-Crusted Oysters paired with a sweet & savory Pomegranate Pineapple Salsa.

Hit Refresh in the New Year

When it comes to the new year, we all have resolutions. But why not use the month of January to reboot? At Travaasa, we're kicking off 2015 with new spa treatments—at both the newly renovated spa at Travaasa Austin and at Hana's spa oasis—that are sure to give your new year a jumpstart.
