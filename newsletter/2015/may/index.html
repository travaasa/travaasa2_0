Expert Tips: Make Time to Nurture Yourself

It's Nurture Month at Travaasa, which means the "me time" everyone has put off for so long, has finally arrived. Whether you're an active outdoorsman, an expert pool-lounger or self-proclaimed introvert, our Spa Director has a few tips for how the make the most of your May. 

Sippin' on Summer

An imperative piece of the summer puzzle is the quintessential refreshing cocktail. In Austin, The Preserve has just rolled out a brand new menu full of them. The new libations are inspired by the seasonal ingredients from the farm and perfect for laying by the pool. Insider tip: we recommend the Lavender Lemonade or Watermelon Cucumber Cooler.

Grill Like Chef Ben

With summer in sight, we're ready for our favorite part of the season—firing up the grill. Whether you're a novice or a grill master, Travaasa Austin's very-own Chef Ben has you covered! Get his step-by-step instructions and recipe for the delicious BBQ Wagyu Beef Ribs. We promise it will be a crowd-pleaser for any Memorial Day celebration.

ON THE HORIZON

June is Man Month

Man Month is an exciting time for us at Travaasa. We've got all the necessary components for the ultimate Mancation—male-focused spa experiences in the Western Sky room, tequila tasting, roping with our cowboys, grilling and, of course, plenty of adventure. July is Independence Month

Nothing says July like grilling and poolside cocktails. Sounds like the perfect time for a trip for one to us! At Travaasa, we're celebrating every aspect of independence for solo travelers. Our experiences offer ways to work on self-reflection, growth, new adventures and, most importantly, R&R.
