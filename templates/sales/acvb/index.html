Dear Meeting Planner,

Please be advised—if you’re looking for the following, you would do well to avoid booking a meeting at Travaasa Austin...

Frozen Steaks?
Nope. Farm-to-table dining is our thing. Meat and produce served at Travaasa come from a variety of Texas farms and ranches within a 60 mile radius. Oh, and our eggs come from our very own chickens.
Trust falls?
Um, no. We’re pretty sure no one ever nodded off while watching their coworkers whip-and-nae-nae on a high ropes challenge course (with zip line), or while throwing hatchets, or while duking it out in a cocktail competition (think Iron Chef, but with alcohol). Trust falls, not so much.
Suits & Pumps?
No gracias. When is the last time you had an authentic conversation in a wool suit? This is Austin, baby—a safe place for boots.
Window-less Meeting Space?
Not even a little bit. We sit on 210 protected acres of the Balcones Canyonlands Preserve, and every meeting space has all-natural lighting with Hill Country views.
Elevators?
Sorry. We know the music is great in there, but we’re more about meandering paths instead of mile-long corridors and bleak, concrete towers.

IF, however, you're looking for a meeting experience outside of the ordinary that your team or client won't soon forget, we'd love to field your proposal request.

Sincerely,
The Travaasa Sales Team
