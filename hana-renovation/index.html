From the moment the historic Hotel Hana-Maui became Travaasa Hana in 2011, our team began its commitment to preserving the authenticity and genuine spirit of aloha that makes Hana such a unique destination to visit. As a continuation of this commitment, in 2015, Travaasa Hana has undergone select renovations to restore and enhance the guest room experience.

For this update, we enlisted the help of Hunton Conrad, the interior designer responsible for the previous iteration of the guest rooms. Hunton has curated a custom collection of pieces that features sustainable, engineered bamboo and coconut wood, simultaneously improving functionality while staying true to Hana's essential character—authentic Hawaii set among stunning outdoor beauty.

I'm happy to announce the refurbishment of the Garden Junior Suites is complete, and the vast majority of work on the Ocean Bungalows (formerly the Sea Ranch Cottages, seen in red), is also complete. Their new lanais offer a spectacular outdoor vantage point to enjoy Hana’s natural beauty.

Work continues on a select number of Ocean Bungalows (seen in gray) that will remain closed for refurbishment through March 2016. All of these rooms will be outfitted with new lanais, furniture and panoramic sliding glass doors as well.
